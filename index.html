About Us

Chroma Shift Interactive is a passionate game development studio focused on creating unique and engaging single-player experiences. We specialize in blending deep life simulation mechanics with the player agency and emergent gameplay found in immersive sims. Our goal is to build worlds that feel alive, where player choices have meaningful consequences and managing fundamental needs is as crucial as exploration or combat.

Our debut title, Odyssey, embodies this philosophy, challenging players to survive and thrive on a detailed island home, navigating intricate social dynamics and environmental challenges within a rich RPG framework.

Our Games

Odyssey (In Development)

Embark on a single-player journey in Odyssey, an immersive RPG simulation set on your island home. More than just quests, it's about survival – manage needs like food, water, sleep, and warmth, which directly impact your abilities and interactions.

Explore a reactive world, build relationships (or rivalries) through a dynamic reputation system, and define your path with a flexible skill system featuring archetypes like Warrior, Thief, Farmer, and more. Your choices shape your adventure in this emergent world of discovery.
